Welcome to Bangalore Palace

The Bangalore Palace, located in Bengaluru, India, is one of the iconic landmarks .
It is a stunning example of Tudor Revival architecture and is steeped in history and royal charm.

Features

  • Central Dome: The palace is adorned with a striking central tower featuring fortified battlements, reminiscent of Tudor-style castles.
  • Gardens: Bangalore Palace is surrounded by beautifully landscaped gardens, providing a serene environment perfect for leisurely walks and photo sessions.
  • Museum and Art Gallery: The interior houses a collection of royal memorabilia, including photographs, paintings, and vintage artifacts from the Wodeyar dynasty.
